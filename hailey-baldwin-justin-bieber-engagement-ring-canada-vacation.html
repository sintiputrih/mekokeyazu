Hailey Baldwin Is on Vacation with Justin Bieber in Canada, Showing Off Her Diamond Ring

Publish date: 2024-01-31

It's just over a month since Hailey Baldwin and Justin Bieber got engaged, but it already feels as though they've been together forever. The pair, who got engaged in the Bahamas, have pretty much been on vacation since the news broke. So it's only fitting that they spent this weekend in Canada eating ice cream, and showing off Hailey's $500,000 ring.

Spotted in Ontario grabbing ice cream with Justin's siblings on Saturday, the newly engaged couple looked completely relaxed hanging out with the fam:

Later in the day they even hung out with a baby pig, because why not?

But perhaps the most exciting part of the whole weekend was this gorgeous close-up of Hailey's engagement ring, which reportedly set Bieber back around half a million dollars:

According to People, Justin picked out the ring with the help of Jack Solow, of New York City’s Solow & Co. store. The jeweler told the publication that Justin wanted a ring "that would gracefully accentuate Hailey’s beautifully shaped hands." Apparently, they eventually decided on "an oval stone that was just exceptional in its length and grace," and as Hailey demonstrates all over social media, the ring looks completely stunning on her.

Bieber and Baldwin were recently seen crying on the streets of New York. Having been on a bike ride together, Baldwin consoled Bieber as he appeared to be weeping, but TMZ reports that the singer was holding a book titled, The Meaning of Marriage: Facing the Complexities of Commitment with the Wisdom of God.
