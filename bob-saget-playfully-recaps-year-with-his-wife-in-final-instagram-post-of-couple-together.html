Bob Saget playfully recaps year with his wife in final Instagram post of couple together

Publish date: 2024-09-16

Bob Saget’s wife, Kelly Rizzo, recapped “another year of fun” in a heart-wrenching final Instagram post of the couple together — with the former “Full House” star counting the months as a series of touching vignettes played.

“Are you ready? Here we go,” Saget, 65, says in the playful video, which shows him with Rizzo, 42, who posted the clip just days before her husband died Sunday.

The post shows the couple engaged in a variety of activities, including cooking together, exercising and kissing.

One snippet shows a faux-mustachioed Saget wearing a motorcycle helmet and Rizzo in a leather jacket.

Another shows him swinging a baseball bat with the caption: “When people feel the need to comment on our 23 year age difference.”

Rizzo also a posted touching photo of them holding hands as she wishes her followers a merry Christmas.

“So happy to spend my very first Christmas with @bobsaget (even though we’ve been married over 3 years!) because he’s finally in Chicago for the holidays!” she wrote.

“Cheers to everyone on this special holiday. May you all be surrounded by peace and love,” Rizzo added.

In another post, she is seen laughing and lipsyncing “Don’t Be Ridiculous.”

“When people say: “Wow, you’re married to a comedian—you must laugh ALL the time!” Rizzo wrote in the clip.

“Comedians are dark and ominous…and yes funny too ???” she captioned the video.

Saget’s family said in a statement to The Post: “We are devastated to confirm that our beloved Bob passed away today.  He was everything to us and we want you to know how much he loved his fans, performing live and bringing people from all walks of life together with laughter.

“Though we ask for privacy at this time, we invite you to join us in remembering the love and laughter that Bob brought to the world,” they added.

Rizzo, a journalist who hosts her own digital series, “Eat Travel Rock TV,” married Saget in 2018.

Florida’s Orange County Sheriff’s Department confirmed on Sunday that Saget was found dead at age 65 in his hotel room at the Ritz-Carlton in Orlando.

According to TMZ, the police and fire departments responded to the hotel around 4 p.m. Sunday after security found the comedian in his room. The comedian was pronounced dead at the scene.

Saget’s cause of death has yet to be revealed.

The former “America’s Funniest Home Videos” host had been on a standup comedy tour and performed a show in Jacksonville, Fla., on Saturday night before his passing.
