Ariel Winters Bare Booty Gets a Touch-Up in Cheeky Photo

Publish date: 2024-02-24

Just a little touch-up! Ariel Winter got cheeky on Instagram on Thursday, September 22, when she shared a revealing behind-the-scenes pic from a photo shoot.

The Modern Family star, 18, posted a black-and-white image of herself appearing to wear nothing but a distressed jean jacket and a pair of pumps as a makeup artist applied a product to her booty. “BUTT really, don’t miss a spot @kipzachary,” she wrote. “#secretshoot #embraceyourcurves.”

Winter has a thing for flaunting her assets for fans on social media. “Everybody is so hyped up on the fact that I post photos where my butt’s out. It’s a butt. Everyone has a butt,” she told Entertainment Tonight in August. “I don’t think it’s crazy.”

She added, “I feel like I’m just having fun. I like my fans to see what I’m doing. … It’s like, ‘Let me live! I’m enjoying my life, you’re enjoying your life. You should be posting your butt if you like it, too!’”

While the Sofia the First voice actress does occasionally respond to negative comments, she mostly tries to brush off people who aren’t into her skin-showing ways. “I’m not going to fight with someone on my Instagram that tells me they don’t like my outfit or I should put my butt away,” she told ET. “I’m sorry you think I should put my butt away, but just unfollow me.”

Her body-positive message goes beyond social media. She proudly puts her curves on display in daisy dukes and cutout dresses, and in February, she left her breast reduction scars uncovered at the 2016 SAG Awards.
