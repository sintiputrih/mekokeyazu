WWE NXT Superstar Amari Miller Tried To End Her Life Last Week

Publish date: 2024-02-05

Amari Miller is currently one of the highlights of NXT’s women’s division, who impressed many fans with her solid in-ring skills. She has been a regular feature on NXT for good reason. Unfortunately, it seems Amari Miller tried to end her life last week.

Amari Miller has established herself as a formidable presence on NXT, impressing audiences with her performances. With her dedication and talent, she is well on her way to becoming a top star.

Amari Miller revealed in a recent TikTok post that she attempted to end her life last week. She revealed the same via a short clip that she shared on her TikTok handle. Miller also urged fans to fight with her as she focused on the importance of mental health.

“Everything isn’t what it seems, mental health is hard, we should all speak out even though I know it’s hard, you are loved, fight through with me plz!🙏🏾 I show this not for sympathy but to relate and know that you are not alone and I’m here for anyone that needs a ear to listen!🥹💪🏾🥰 #mentalhealth #blessed #fyp #stayalive #pushthrough #positivity.”

Mental health is a very important matter and should be taken seriously as much as possible. We here at Ringside News send our best wishes and support to Amari Miller and hope she gets the help she needs.
