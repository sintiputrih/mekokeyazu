Meet Benoye And Dorothy Williams!

Publish date: 2024-01-13

Married to Medicine returned for season 10 in 2023, and one of the returning cast is Heavenly Kimes, an Atlanta-based dentist. Her parents are reportedly named Benoye and Dorothy Williams. Who were her folks? How long were they married? Scroll down this article to read all about them.

Who Are Heavenly Kimes Parents?

Heavenly Kimes, a leading Atlanta dentist, was born to her parents Benoye Williams and Dorothy Williams.

Born on November 17, 1970, Heavenly attended Florida A&M University earning a B.S. in Chemistry and Military Science, and then moved on to Meharry Medical College, where she graduated in the top 10% of her class with a Doctorate of Dental Surgery.

Dr. Heavenly Kimes is a relationship guru, business titan, motivational speaker, and author. Heading Smiles by Dr. Heavenly, a thriving cosmetic dental office in Atlanta, Dr. Kimes is a specialist in veneers, full arch, single implants, and more. Dr. Kimes is a member of the Georgia Dental Society, the American Academy of General Dentistry, the Academy of Implant Dentistry, the National Dental Association, and Meharry Medical College, where she graduated first in her class with a doctorate in dental surgery. She also holds a bachelor’s degree in biology from Florida A&M University, with minors in chemistry and military science.

After completing dental school, she attended an AEGD program in Memphis TN. She then worked as an Associate Dentist for over 3 years. With over 20 years of dental experience, Dr. Kimes is a TOP CHOICE for all your dental needs.

Heavenly is married to her husband  Dr. Damon Kimes. Damon, age 53, was born on 15 July 1970.

They have been married for more than 20 years now. The couple share three kids and three dogs.

Their children are named Alaura Kimes, Damon Kimes Jr, and Zachary Kimes.

Meet Benoye Williams, Heavenly Kimes Father

Heavenly Kimes’ father is called Benoye Williams. He passed away a while back but we weren’t able to locate his obituary.

Benoye was a Vietnam veteran. In November 2023, Heavenly wished her dad, “My father Benoye Williams. Is actually a Vietnam Veteran! I thank him for his Service!! #married2med #DrHeavenly. Happy Veterans Day #TeamDaddy.”

Likewise, on Father’s Day, Benoye wrote, “Happy Father’s Day to my Dad Benoye Williams. May he rest in peace!! #Ben #Dad #happyfathersday #DrHeavenly #Married2med #TeamDaddy.”

Meet Dorothy Williams, Heavenly Kimes Mother

Heavenly Kimes’s mother is reportedly named Dorothy Williams. Dorothy was in October 1935, however, she is no more.

In January 2022, Heavenly wrote, “Thank you all for the. Calls, texts, Well wishes., And flowers! It is appreciated! It’s always touching to know so many people are there for me praying. R.I.P momma we love you! #DrHeavenly #DrJackie #MsQuad #married2med #TeamDaddy.”

On a memorable occasion at the Married to Medicine dentist’s home, Dr. Jackie Walters and Quad Webb consoled their best friend in her hour of need after the death of Dr. Heavenly’s mother. “My friends came over. They brought me some well wishes. They brought me some candles and some books,” Dr. Heavenly shared in a video. “I got me some positive thinking books. Thank you, Ms. Quad. Everything. Thank you, Jackie, for the candles, because that’s my favorite thing.”

“I wanted to thank everybody for all of their well wishes, you know, the condolences, the flowers, the candy, everything. Let me show the flowers and everything we got,” Dr. Heavenly spoke while displaying every present she had received as a token of condolence from others. “Thank y’all so much. Y’all made this so much easier, and I appreciate all y’all. Y’all are beautiful people. Thank you.”

Heavenly shared that she recently broke up and it was downhill from there. Dorothy was 89 when she passed away.

Before her passing, Dorothy’s illness also prompted Dr. Contessa to make peace with Heavenly. However, Dorothy was assumed to be very abusive herself as she has been alleged that she used to call Heavenly “B*tc*.”

On Mother’s Day 2022, he added, “Happy Mother’s Day !! Remembering my mom !! R.I.P. and being the best mother I can be #memories #what life is about #married2med #DrHeavenly #TeamDaddy.”
