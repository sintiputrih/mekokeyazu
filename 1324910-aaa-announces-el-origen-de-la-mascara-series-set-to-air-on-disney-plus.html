AAA Announces El Origen De La Mascara, Series Set To Air On Disney Plus

Publish date: 2024-05-29

‘s collaboration with Marvel is expanding

A trailer for the upcoming Marvel Lucha Libre show, titled “El Origen De La Mascara”, aired during the pre-show of TripleMania XXX: CDMX on October 15. The program is coming to Disney Plus, and it will premiere on December 21, 2022. This will be the show from a first wrestling promotion that will air on Disney Plus. As of this writing, it’s unclear whether the program will air exclusively in Mexico or worldwide.

AAA and Marvel have been working together since 2020. The “Marvel Lucha Libre Edition” has allowed AAA to featured in-ring performers with characters that are inspired by Marvel’s iconic heroes and villains. Some examples include El Aracno (Spider-Man), and El Leyenda Americana (Captain America), El Terror Purpura (Thanos) and El Venenoide (Venom).
