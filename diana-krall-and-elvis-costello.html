About

Diana Krall is a 59 year old Canadian Jazz Musician. Born Diana Jean Krall on 16th November, 1964 in Nanaimo, British Columbia, Canada, she is famous for Justin Time (1993) in a career that spans 1993–present. Her zodiac sign is Scorpio.
