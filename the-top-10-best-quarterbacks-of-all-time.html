The Top 10 Best Quarterbacks of All-Time

Publish date: 2024-02-14

When it comes to the NFL, the quarterback position is arguably the most crucial on the field. Over the years, the league has seen several exceptional quarterbacks who have left an indelible mark on the sport.

Here, we delve into the careers and achievements of the top 10 best quarterbacks of all time, taking into account their skills, impact on the game, and overall contribution to the sport.

1. Joe Montana

Achievements:

  • Four-time Super Bowl champion
  • Three-time Super Bowl MVP
  • Eight-time Pro Bowl selection

Skills and Impact: Joe Montana is renowned for his remarkable accuracy, leadership, and ability to perform under pressure. His uncanny ability to orchestrate game-winning drives earned him the nickname "Joe Cool."

Montana's impact on the game is evident through his numerous records and his role in establishing the San Francisco 49ers as a football powerhouse during the 1980s.

2. Tom Brady

Achievements:

  • Seven-time Super Bowl champion
  • Five-time Super Bowl MVP
  • Three-time NFL MVP

Skills and Impact: Tom Brady's career is synonymous with excellence and longevity. His work ethic, football IQ, and clutch performances have solidified his place as one of the greatest quarterbacks of all time.

Brady's impact extends beyond his on-field performance, as he has redefined the parameters of success and age in the NFL.

3. Peyton Manning

Achievements:

  • Two-time Super Bowl champion
  • Five-time NFL MVP
  • 14-time Pro Bowl selection

Skills and Impact: Peyton Manning's cerebral approach to the game, combined with his unparalleled work ethic, revolutionized the quarterback position.

Known for his ability to read defenses and make adjustments at the line of scrimmage, Manning's impact is felt in the way modern quarterbacks approach the mental aspect of the game.

4. Johnny Unitas

Achievements:

  • Three-time NFL champion
  • Three-time NFL MVP
  • 10-time Pro Bowl selection

Skills and Impact: Johnny Unitas was a trailblazer in every sense, revolutionizing the quarterback position with his downfield passing and late-game heroics.

His impact on the game can be seen in his influence on future generations of quarterbacks and the enduring legacy he left with the Baltimore Colts.

5. Dan Marino

Achievements:

  • NFL MVP
  • Nine-time Pro Bowl selection
  • First quarterback to pass for over 5,000 yards in a season

Skills and Impact: Dan Marino's quick release and prolific passing set new standards for the quarterback position.

Despite never winning a Super Bowl, Marino's impact on the game is undeniable, as he redefined the passing game and left an indelible mark on the Miami Dolphins and the NFL as a whole.

6. Brett Favre

Achievements:

  • Super Bowl champion
  • Three-time NFL MVP
  • 11-time Pro Bowl selection

Skills and Impact: Brett Favre's gunslinger mentality, durability, and passion for the game endeared him to fans and solidified his place as one of the most iconic quarterbacks in NFL history.

His impact on the game is evident through his numerous records and his role in revitalizing the Green Bay Packers during his tenure.

7. John Elway

Achievements:

  • Two-time Super Bowl champion
  • NFL MVP
  • Nine-time Pro Bowl selection

Skills and Impact: John Elway's athleticism, arm strength, and ability to perform in high-pressure situations made him a transcendent figure in the NFL. 

8. Otto Graham

Achievements:

  • Seven-time NFL champion
  • Three-time NFL MVP
  • Five-time Pro Bowl selection

Skills and Impact: Otto Graham's dominance in the early years of the NFL laid the groundwork for future generations of quarterbacks.

His impact on the game is immortalized through his unparalleled success with the Cleveland Browns and his innovative approach to the quarterback position.

9. Steve Young

Achievements:

  • Three-time Super Bowl champion
  • Two-time NFL MVP
  • Seven-time Pro Bowl selection

Skills and Impact: Steve Young's dual-threat abilities and exceptional efficiency as a passer set him apart in his era.

His impact on the game can be seen in his seamless succession of Joe Montana and his instrumental role in the success of the San Francisco 49ers in the 1990s.

10. Aaron Rodgers

Achievements:

  • Super Bowl champion
  • Three-time NFL MVP
  • Nine-time Pro Bowl selection

Skills and Impact: Aaron Rodgers' arm talent, creativity, and ability to extend plays have redefined the quarterback position in the modern era. 

 

The top 10 best quarterbacks of all time have not only left an indelible mark on the NFL but have also redefined the parameters of success, skill, and impact on the game. Their contributions have shaped the league and inspired future generations of quarterbacks, solidifying their places in football history.
