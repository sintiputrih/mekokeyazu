Jenna Ortega (born September 27, 2002) is an American Actress and Social Media Personality from Coachella Valley, Palm Desert, California. She is popular for her lead role in “Jane the Virgin” as the young Jane on The CW.

Jenna also gained fame for her role in the Disney show named “Stuck in the Middle” as Harley Diaz. Furthermore, the 16-year-old actress started her career with the small TV role in the series “Rob” in 2012.

The actress made a debut on the silver screen with the movie named Iron Man 3 as Vice President’s daughter. Moreover, Ortega also gave her voice of Princess Isabel in the “Elena of Avalor” (Disney Channel animated television series).

Some of her other notable works are Rake, Richie Rich, After Words etc. She is also extremely popular on Instagram having 4.4+ million followers on the platform.
