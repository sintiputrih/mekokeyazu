Kylie Jenner shares view of the Aspen home she's renting with Kendall and Kris for $450K/MONTH

Publish date: 2024-01-14

Kylie Jenner has given her followers a sneak peek at the stunning seven-bedroom Aspen home she’s staying in , which is setting her back a huge $450k-a-month. 

She arrived in Aspen on her private jet on Tuesday, accompanied by partner Travis Scott, 28, sister Kendall Jenner, 25, mom Kris Jenner, 65, her boyfriend Corey Gamble, 40, pal Fai Khadra, 29 and daughter Stormi, two.

On Sunday, the cosmetics mogul, 23, shared behind-the-scenes views of the four-storey Colorado vacation home, also available at a cost of $25,000 for daily rentals which has a theater, swimming pool and bowling alley.

The massive home, built in 2020, covers 20,000sq ft with seven bedrooms, seven full bathrooms and half bathrooms.

There are plenty of spots for recreation without going out into the cold, as the mansion includes a long indoor swimming pool and a bowling lane.

There’s also an expansive deck and a patio with gorgeous views of the snow-covered hills.
