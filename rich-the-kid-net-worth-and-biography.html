Rich The Kid Biography

Born Dimitri Leslie Roger professionally known as Rich The Kid is an American singer-songwriter and entertainer who was born and bred in the United States Of America. Rich The Kid was born in Queens on July 13, 1992. He is of Haitian descent, His parents divorce when he was still very young.

After his parents’ divorce, He moved with his mother to Woodstock, Georgia, a suburb north of Atlanta, when he was 13 years old.

According to Rich the Kid, He got his music influence listening to the likes of Nas, Jay-Z, 2Pac, Notorious B.I.G. & 50 Cent, but after moving to Woodstock, he started listening to T.I. and Young Jeezy.

He premiered into the music industry with the stage name Black Boy Da Kid, which he would later changed to Rich the Kid. He attended Elmont Memorial Junior – Senior High School in Elmont, New York.

Rich The Kid Career.

Rich The Kid Developed interest for music at a very tender age, He started his music career at a very tender but debuted the American music industry somewhere around 2011, In 2013, he released his debut solo mixtape, Been About the Benjamins, He also went further to released a collaborative mixtape series with Migos called Streets On Lock (volume 1 and 2), with volume 3 being released in 2014 and volume 4 being released in 2015.

So far, He has been making wave in the world entertainment industry, with over three studio album, He has sold out over over 2 million copies since he began his musical career, He music has topped many chart in US, UK and Canada. In 2016 he launched his own label called Rich Forever Music. The first artist he signed to the label was Famous Dex. In 2017 he signed a deal with Interscope Records.
