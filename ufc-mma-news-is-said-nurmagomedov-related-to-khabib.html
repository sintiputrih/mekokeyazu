Is Said Nurmagomedov Related to Khabib?

Publish date: 2024-05-24

UFC’s former lightweight champion, Khabib Nurmagomedov, has several cousins and friends from Dagestan in MMA. In fact, fight fans may misidentify many of his teammates as them being related to him because of their similar looks. One such fighter is Said Nurmagomedov. Considering they have the same last name, many assume they may be brothers. Are they related, though?

All about Said Nurmagomdev

Said Nurmagomedov is a mixed martial artist born on April 5, 1990 Makhachkala, Dagestan, Russia. He made his MMA debut at the nascent age of 15 in 2009, facing a 4-0 record holder Oscar Nave and defeated him in the opening round with an armbar. He has since fought in various MMA promotions and eventually made his debut in Dana White’s promotion in 2018 and now competes in the bantamweight division of the UFC. He has a professional MMA record 17-2-0 and has 6 wins and 1 loss in his UFC tenure so far, with notable victories over fighters such as Ricardo Ramos and Mark Striegl.

Nurmagomedov is known for his aggressive fighting style, powerful striking, and high-level grappling skills, which he developed through years of training in sambo and wrestling. He made his UFC debut in 2018, and since then, he has been steadily climbing the ranks of the bantamweight division, with his sights set on becoming a UFC champion.

Is said Nurmagomedov related to Khabib?

Said Nurmagomedov, a rising prospect in the UFC, is often confused with the famous undefeated UFC legend Khabib Nurmagomedov. Due to their identical surnames and shared Dagestani roots, many have speculated that the two fighters are related. The rumors gained momentum after Said signed with the UFC in early 2018, leading to a lot of speculation in the media. But Said Nurmagomedov and Khabib Nurmagomedov’s relationship doesn’t exceed beyond their native land Dagestan and coincidentally identical last names.

As reported by Vimbuzz, to address the rumors, both fighters have spoken publicly and made it clear that they are not related in any way. Khabib himself spoke about it in an interview, stating, “Of course, I know him. He’s a very good friend. He’s an amazing fighter. But he’s not my cousin even though we have the same name. He has a very big chance in the UFC.”

Said Nurmagomedov has also addressed the issue and said, “It happens frequently. Even in Abu Dhabi, I get that. Not Khabib specifically, but they think we’re all the same. But it doesn’t bother me, though”.

Therefore, it is clear that despite their shared surname and Dagestani heritage, UFC 135-pounder Said Nurmagomedov and Khabib Nurmagomedov are not related in any manner. Despite the confusion, Said has made a name for himself in the UFC with his impressive skills and has a promising career ahead of him.
