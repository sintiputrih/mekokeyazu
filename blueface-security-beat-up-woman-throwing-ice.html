Blueface Tells Security To Beat Up Fan For Ice-Throwing Incident

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Blueface‘s recent show turned violent after a woman in attendance allegedly threw ice at the rapper’s on-again/off-again fiancée, Jaidyn Alexis.

Taking place in the Los Angeles area on Saturday (December 16), footage shows Blue calling a blonde-haired woman on stage after ice flew Jaidyn’s way. Once she made her way up, she denied she was the person behind the throw – but the “Thotiana” rapper responded by saying: “Oh you didn’t throw nothing? You the only one with a cup of ice.”
