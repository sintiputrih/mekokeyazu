Zsela Thompson- Biography

Zsela Thompson is best known as Tessa Thompson’s half-sister. She is an actress best known for her work on Veronica Mars as Jackie Cook and as Bianca in the film Creed. Zsela went on to play Valkyrie in Thor: Ragnarok and Agent M in Men in Black: International. She also appeared in For Colored Girls, Dear White People, and Westworld.

Zsela Thompson- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Zsela’s father is the black singer-songwriter Marc Anthony Thompson, who founded the musical collective Chocolate Genius, Inc.Her mother is of Mexican and European descent.

Thompson went to Santa Monica High School, where she played Hermia in a student production of A Midsummer Night’s Dream, and Santa Monica College (SMC), where she majored in cultural anthropology. She also attended lectures by Lisa Wolpe of the Los Angeles Women’s Shakespeare Company while at SMC (LAWSC).

Body Measurements: Height, Weight, Hair color

She is 5 feet 5 inches tall, or 1.65 meters or 165 centimeters. Zsela weighs approximately 55 kg (121 pounds). She has lovely dark brown eyes and blonde hair.

Quick Facts

  • Tessa Thompson, Zsela Thompson’s half-sister, has been in the drama series Copper (2012-2013), the noir drama series Veronica Mars (2005-2006), and the science fiction series Westworld (2016-present).
  • Sylvie’s Love earned her a Primetime Emmy nomination.
  • She has also gained critical acclaim for her roles in independent films such as Sorry to Bother You (2018), Little Woods (2018), Annihilation (2018), Sylvie’s Love (2020), and Passing (2021).
