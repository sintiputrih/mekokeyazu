Dak Prescott and Sarah Jane Ramos have a first child on the way

‘Pregnancy alert‘! Dak Prescott is expanding his family with his girlfriend Sarah Jane Ramos.

On Saturday, November 24, the pregnant mama shared a new Instagram post sharing the happy news that she is expecting her first child with his footballer boyfriend.

She posted a series of herself from the maternity photoshoots where she proudly displayed her growing baby bump. She also wrote a caption alongside the photos revealing the sex of the baby.

“A little bit of Heaven sent down to Eart Our Immeasurable blessing. I cannot wait to raise a strong, confident, beautiful baby girl with you @_4dak,”

The Dallas Cowboys quarterback, 30, was the first to comment on his pregnant girlfriend’s post,

“How thankful I am to do this with you can’t be explained! God makes no mistakes and nothing is coincidental and for that, I thank Him Daily! I love you, and y’all can always count on me Let’s do this Mama”

He also reshared the post on his Instagram story and wrote next to the pictures, ‘Beyond Blessed!” and “Girl Dad Incoming.”

The pregnancy news comes just a week after the couple confirmed their relationship.

On her 30th birthday, Sarah made her relationship Instagram official with Dak by posting a picture of the two of them from the birthday bash.

Dak Prescott and Sarah Jane Ramos: Dating life

It’s unclear how long Dak and Sarah had been together.

However, they may have started dating after the NFL player and his two-year girlfriend, Natalie Buffett, broke up in March 2022.

Ramos posted a picture of them hanging together during the opening week of the NFL season, which sparked dating rumors early in September.

She was then spotted at Prescott’s nephew’s Halloween party and shared stories about her Cowboys fanaticism, notably after their victory over the New England Patriots.

In August, when the host Joe Trahan questioned asked about his relationship status during an interview with WFAA, he simply replied,

“it is pending”
