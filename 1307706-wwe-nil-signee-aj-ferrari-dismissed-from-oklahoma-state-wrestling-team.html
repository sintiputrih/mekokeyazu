WWE NIL Signee AJ Ferrari Dismissed From Oklahoma State Wrestling Team

Publish date: 2024-07-27

AJ Ferrari might be part of ‘s Next In Line program, but he has been cut from the Oklahoma State wrestling team.

The O’Colly reported the news, and OSU confirmed it to Pistols Firing.

A Stillwater woman filed a protective order against Ferrari on July 5, but the investigation is ongoing, so the official records have not been disclosed. The Stillwater Police Department is conducting an investigation, led by its Criminal Investigations Department, into a report of sexual assault that was made against Ferrari

As a wrestler, Ferrari was 30-1 with Oklahoma State. A car accident ended his sophomore season with a 10-0 record.

WWE announced Ferrari as a member of the inaugural NIL Class in December 2021.

WrestleZone will provide more details as they become available.
