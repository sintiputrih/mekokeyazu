Who is koichi hirose?

The deuteragonist of the movie Diamond is Unbreakable, Koichi Hirose (Guang Lai Kang Yi – Hirose Koichi), also appears briefly in Vento Aureo.

Koichi, a reserved freshman in high school, befriends Josuke Higashikata and finds himself drawn into the community of Stand users.

Koichi can use echoes and is a Stand User.

Even though he is sometimes portrayed as being shorter, Koichi is an adolescent who is 157 cm (5’2″) tall and of average frame. Koichi is wearing casual clothing instead of his standard school uniform.

In t he beginning, Koichi keeps his hair light and neatly combed back. When he fights with Tamami and Yukako, his hair has a propensity to spike up when he becomes excited (producing the look that is like that of Dragon Ball Z character Gohan in Super Saiyan 2 during the Cell Games). The character cuts his hair, resulting in flat-tops near the end of his encounter with Yukako, which later turn into spikes when he faces Yoshikage Kira. His hair can be any hue, with blue, blond, or silver being the most popular choices across all mediums.

Baby koichi

Koichi was covered in long hair on the day he was born. Although his parents were excited to see what colour it would be, it was silver white. His hair became more wavy and lighter in colour as he aged. He had the loveliest little spikes when he was a baby, which was a long time ago.

Childhood koichi

Koichi had long, untamed hair as a child that was covered in spikes. His mother used to tie his hair up in a sweet topknot, which would then fall out and stick out in all directions. His hair got longer and easier to manage as he got older.
