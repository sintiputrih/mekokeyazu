The Real Reason Why Lindsay Lohan Lives In Dubai

Publish date: 2024-06-29

After spending almost her entire life in the spotlight, Lindsay Lohan was eager for a change of pace. "Moving here was a fresh start," she told Emirates Woman in 2018. She enjoyed how the focus of conversations was not centered on celebrity life. "You hear more about real current events rather than celebrity gossip, which I really appreciate," she told the publication.

Avoiding public scrutiny was a major motivating factor in The Parent Trap star's decision. "I moved here for that purpose — I don't have to be publicly seen all the time, or discuss what I'm doing," she told Emirates Woman. Enjoying a level of anonymity was important to Lohan. "There's a certain calmness that I find [in Dubai]. There's no paparazzi, no cameras; that's a big deal for me," she said in her 2018 interview with W Magazine.

Ultimately, the serenity that Lohan found since moving to Dubai has allowed her to be more productive. "I get more done because I don't have the scrutiny and fixation on what I'm doing every second. I work all the time; my mind never stops," she told Emirates Woman.

On Jul. 02, 2020, Lohan celebrated turning 34-years-old, and in an Instagram video post she showed off her personal growth. When reflecting on her low-key birthday she realized, "How lucky we all are to just have the people around us that we love and the people in our lives that we love and how simple is key."
