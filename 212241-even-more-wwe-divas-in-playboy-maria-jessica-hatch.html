Maryse Ouellet is the model for January of Playboy Hottest nos 2008 Calendar.

Just wanted to pass along that The Bussey Bunch will be airing on The Learning Channel and premiers January 22nd at 9pm. Regardless if I make the final cut (it’s up to the network) you all should check out the show.
