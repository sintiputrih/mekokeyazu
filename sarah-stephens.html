Career

She got a two-year deal with Chic Model Management after winning the countrywide ‘Girlfriend’ Magazine Model Search in 2006. She was then signed by Next Model Management in New York and Paris. She has been on the covers of several publications, including Rush Australia, Frankie magazine, Madison, and Eurowoman. She has appeared in editorial spreads in Russian, Japanese, and Italian magazines, as well as Harper’s Bazaar (Australia), Harper’s Bazaar (en Espaol), Exit magazine, and Karen magazine.

She has also appeared in international advertising campaigns for Lacoste, Forever New, Jag, Wrangler, Just Jeans, Ksubi, Levi’s, Lisa Ho, Metalicus, Review Australia, and others. She walked 18 shows during her first Australian Fashion Week in 2007. In early 2008, she met photographer Ellen von Unwerth in Paris, and she was photographed for editorial spreads in Russian Vogue, Mixtemagazine, and Above magazine. During her June 2008 visit to New York, she was photographed by Greg Kadel, who shot editorials with Stephens for Numéro (France), Numéro Homme, and Italian Vogue.

She walked 14 shows at her first New York Fashion Week in September 2008. She is most recognized in the United States for becoming the youngest model to walk in the annual Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show in Miami in 2008, a few months after her 18th birthday. Sarah is most recognized in Europe as the face of Lacoste’s “Love of Pink” fragrance. She appeared in the 2015 horror film ‘The Witch as a teenage witch and subsequently in the 2017 song video ‘The Other’ by singer Lauv.
