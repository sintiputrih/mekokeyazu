Kurupts Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-23

The estimated net worth of Kurupt is approximately $1 Million


Kurupt’s Background

Ricardo Emmanuel Brown, known by his stage name Kurupt, was born on November 23rd, 1972, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA. He is a renowned rapper, singer, and actor, most famous for his association with the rap group “The Dog Pound”. Between 2002 and 2005, he served as the Vice President of Death Row Records. Kurupt is also a member of the hip hop group “The HRSMN”.

Kurupt’s net worth has been estimated at $1 million in 2015, primarily earned through his music career, television appearances, and other personal ventures. His wealth has seen significant growth, as his net worth in 2011 was approximately $500,000. His music earnings were supplemented with income from movie appearances, endorsements, and a personal marijuana line called “Moon Rocks”.

Kurupt’s Music and Acting Career

Kurupt initiated his music career in California after moving from Pennsylvania. He gained prominence after signing with Death Row Records in the early ’90s. His collaborations with renowned artists and his album “Dogg Food” contributed significantly to his wealth. As a rapper, he was part of the well-known “East Coast West Coast war”, a hip hop rivalry in the 1990s.

He ventured into acting with appearances in several movies and television series, including “Murder Was the Case”, “The Wash”, “Hollywood Homicide”, “Brothers in Arms”, and “The Penthouse”. Kurupt’s acting career has contributed substantially to his net worth, with earnings surpassing $300,000.

In his personal life, Kurupt had relationships with model and actress Foxy Brown and singer Natina Reed. He has been married to rapper Gail Gotti since 2007.
