Career

In 2000, he published his debut album with bandmate Llandel Malave. El Sobreviviente, his debut solo album, was released in 2004. Wisin releases his second solo studio album, El Regreso del Sobreviviente, ten years later in 2014. Wisin is presently on tour with Prince Royce as part of their Power and Love project. Wisin and Yandel first met in school. In the late 1990s, they became close friends and started performing as a duet.

They ultimately released their first album, Los Reyes del Nuevo Milenio, in 2000. They then sought to break through to a big label with Mi Vida… My Life, their debut for Universal subsidiary Machete Music in 2003. Then, in 2005, they published Pa’l Mundo, which became a mainstream hit. Wisin and Yandel then launched their own label, WY Records, and introduced an associate group, Los Vaqueros. The pair took on a sabbatical in November 2013, after their Lderes tour, although despite speculations that their separation was due to disagreements, Yandel disputed such claims and clarified that they had not dissolved.
