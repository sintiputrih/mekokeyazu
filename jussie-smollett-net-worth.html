Jussie Smollett | Career

Jussie Smollett started his acting career as a child model in NYC, eventually appearing as an extra in the movies “Mo’ Better Blues” (1990) and “New Jack City” (1991).

Jussie starred with his five real-life siblings in the comedy series “On Our Own” (1994–1995).

Similarly, Smollett made his acting comeback in 2012, starring in Patrik-Ian Polk’s LGBTQ comedy-drama “The Skinny.”

In the same year, he published “The Poisoned Hearts Club,” an EP.

Later on, he appeared as a guest star on “The Mindy Project” (2012) and “Revenge” (2014).

Smollett was cast as a guest star in “Underground,” which premiered in 2016, featuring his younger sister Jurnee in 2015.

“Sum of My Music,” Smollett’s debut album, was released in 2018. The album was an R&B album that incorporated electronic and hip-hop influences.
