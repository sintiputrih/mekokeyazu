Who are Piers Morgans children? Spencer, Stanley, Albert, Elsie Morgan

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Piers Morgan is an English television presenter and journalist and is popular for his controversial and aggressive style of breaking stories on television.

He was a co-presenter on Good Morning Britain and has recently launched his show, Piers Morgan Uncensored.

Let’s get upclose and personal with Piers Morgan.

How many children does Piers Morgan have?

Piers Morgan is a father to four kids.

Spencer, Stanley, and Albert were the three sons of the TV host’s first marriage to Marion Shalloe.

Elise was born in 2011 after Celia Walden, a fellow journalist, and Elise were married.

Spencer Morgan 

Elise was born in 2011 after Celia Walden, a fellow journalist, and Elise were married.

The 28-year-old, who was born in July 1993, is accustomed to the limelight.

Like his father, he enjoys taking selfies with famous people, and he is well-known for hanging out with reality TV stars like Mark Wright, Niall Horan, and Georgia Toffolo.

Spencer gained notoriety in 2019 after making a bogus declaration that he will be moving into the Love Island villa.

Piers frequently claims that the show constantly approaches his boys about appearing on their matchmaking series.

Speaking on GMB at the time he said: “My sons get approached for these shows but they know I’ll write them out of the will if they go on.”

Spencer backed his father following his exit from Good Morning Britain, posting a picture on his Instagram with the caption: “Team Piers. Always, forever.”

He is often seen on various holidays and enjoying time at Pier’s second home in Beverly Hills

Stanley Morgan

Stanley, 24, is an actor.

Back in 2019, fans of Piers have previously urged Stanley to sign up for Love Island, after he shared a topless snap of himself on social media.

Stanley is seen flexing his muscles in a pair of swimming trunks beside a table tennis table.

Piers penned the caption: “Nice guns son – shame you still lost 21-10 to the old man.”

One follower gushed: “Phoawwww is he single” while another suggested: “He should go on Love Island.”

Stanley is a co-founder and creative director at the independent film firm One Five Five Productions, though out of the spotlight.

In January 2021, Piers happily screened his son’s short film on loneliness and praised the “strong things” in the sequences.

Hussain Manawer, a prominent poet and mental health advocate, is included in the moving passages, which were directed by Stanley.

Albert Morgan

Piers’ youngest son Albert, 21, was revealed to have had symptoms of coronavirus in April 2020.

The presenter shocked his former GMB co-host Susanna Reid with the admission on the ITV breakfast show

Piers said: “One of my sons had it in a much milder way. My youngest boy.

“I think most people are associating (lack of taste and smell) that with the virus, it’s a very unnerving thing to get.”

Albert, often known as Bertie, finished his A-Levels in 2019. He shares his father’s passion for the football team Arsenal and frequently talks about the Gunners on social media.

Piers stated in 2018 that Bertie was taken to the hospital right away after dislocating his shoulder during a rugby match.

Bertie, who has over 6,000 followers on Twitter and 13.4k followers on Instagram, deftly avoids the spotlight.

Elise Morgan

Elise, Piers’ first daughter and the youngest child in the family, was born on November 25, 2011.

She would then be ten years old.

She is also the sole child of the presenter and his second wife, Celia Walden.

Elise appeared to be unfazed by the camera, much like her well-known father, as she smiled broadly while posing for photos with her family in Los Angeles, California, in 2020.
