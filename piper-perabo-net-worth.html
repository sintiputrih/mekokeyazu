Piper Perabo Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Piper Perabo is an American stage, film, and television actress with a net worth of $10 million. Piper starred in the drama “Covert Affairs” from 2010 to 2014. Piper Perabo was paid $75,000 per episode of the show.

She was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Television Series Drama for her work on “Covert Affairs.” From 2010 to 2014, the show aired for five seasons.

In 2019, she starred in the action film “Angel Has Fallen” as Leah Banning. Perabo appeared as Linda Craft in Showtime’s supernatural horror series “Penny Dreadful: City of Angels” in 2020. She was in six episodes of the show.

She also co-starred as Angela in the science fiction fantasy film “Spontaneous” in 2020. Perabo joined the cast of Yellowstone as Summer Higgins, an animal rights activist and ally of John in season 4.
