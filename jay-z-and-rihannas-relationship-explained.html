Jay-Z And Rihanna's Relationship Explained

Publish date: 2024-05-13

Rihanna admits to having a tough time when she first signed with Def Jam. And the leading cause of it: Rumors. "At first, it was funny. Then it was frustrating. Because it got really intense where people just weren't letting up," Rihanna told The Guardian. "Then it turned into a 'beef' between me and Beyoncé. I was like, 'Where are these people going with this? This is crazy.'" Rihanna insisted that Beyoncé's always been "supportive," adding, "She's so great."

In 2010, Rihanna gushed to Tyra Banks about her first Bey encounter. "I could not speak," she admitted. "That's somebody I really looked up to for a long time." Rihanna said Beyoncé passed on some good advice, telling her "to always have fun and be in control."

Rihanna also confessed that she dreams of Beyoncé getting into her undies. "I mean, Beyoncé got body," she told E! News when asked who she would like to wear her lingerie brand, Savage. "Beyoncé in anybody's panties gonna set it off, but I would rather them be mine." In case of any lingering doubts, Rihanna shot dead the Beyoncé feud rumors once again, admitting she isn't here for made-up drama. "Here's the deal, [gossips] just get so excited to feast on something that's negative. Something that's competitive," she told Vogue. "Something that's, you know, a rivalry. And that's just not what I wake up to."
