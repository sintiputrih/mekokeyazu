Elsa is an invented cartoon character who is the Queen of Arendelle. Elsa is the character that was presented in the world’s popular amazing cartoon movie “Frozen” and its continuation “Frozen II”. Moreover, Elsa has gained praise and positive comments widely from the viewers of the movie.

Early Life and Childhood

According to the inventors of the cartoon character, Elsa’s birthdate is 22nd December 1818 and her birthplace is Arendelle. The names of her father and mother are King Agnarr and Queen Iduna. Also, the name of her paternal grandparents is King Runeard and Queen Rita.

Similarly, she even has a younger sister whose name is Princess Anna. The interesting fact about her is that she has had some magical powers from the day she was born. Her power was granted by the spirits of the Enchanted Forest. However, this information was shown only in the second part of the movie “Frozen”.

At the beginning part of the movie, it was shown that she was hidden from the world and kept inside a room as she was unable to control her magical powers. So, she was always living with the fear of hurting her sister and the world from her uncontrollable magical powers. She started fearing after an incident where she nearly killed her sister unintentionally. In the childhood days of her and Anna, Anna woke her early in the morning. Then, they play together and even ask her to create ice.

While they are playing, Anna tends to fall, and to save Anna from falling, she takes action to save her. But she accidentally hits her with ice which goes through Anna’s head and makes Anna unconscious. Then, their parents arrive there and take Anna to the trolls who are in the magical forest. The leader of the troll family however helps Anna by erasing her memory which remembers her powers from her head. They also warn Anna’s family to keep her heart safe as if it is hit then it will be difficult to recover Anna.

Special Features

The head troll advises her to learn to use and control her magical powers. He also adds not be afraid of her powers. The reason is that fear is the only component that will lead Elsa to be unable to control her powers. After that, her father, King Agnarr tells her to “conceal don’t feel”. From that day, She keeps herself far from everyone adding her sister Anna, and starts living in her room. Anna stays in the room without stepping outside a single step and spends all her time remembering the days when she used to play with her sister.

Then, at the time of the coronation, all the Arendelle and other people from several parts of the world go to the Kingdom. Also, she who was going to be the new queen there gets worried due to a debate between her and her sister puts Arendelle in Eternal Winter, and leaves. After that, Anna goes on a journey to break the ice and the winter season after discussing it with her sister.

At last, she finally gets to know how to use and control her magical powers properly. In part 2 of Frozen, she becomes more independent and fearless. She even turns out to become one of the spirits of the enchanted forest.

Voice

The voice actor of her character was Megan Mullally. Later, she was changed by Broadway actress and singer Idina Menzel. Idina Menzel is more popular for her performance of Elphaba from “Wicked”. The singer and actor gave an audition for the main role in the Disney animated feature movie, “Tangled” in the year 2010. However, she failed to get the role but instead cast the director recorded her voice and later revealed the recording to the executives of the movie, Frozen.

The singer was very amazed when she got a chance to give an audition. She was successful this time and got the character after reading out the script. Chris Buck who was the director was sure that Idina Menzel’s voice would promote in the description of the role, saying, “Idina has a sense of vulnerability in her voice. She plays a very strong character, but someone who lives in fear – so we needed someone who could portray both sides of the character, and Idina was just amazing.” In an interview with Menzel, she talked about the common features of Elsa and Elphaba.

Menzel also said that both of them are very powerful and were misunderstanding people. Likewise, Menzel herself was more similar to the role because just like else he was hiding her amazing skills which were her singing talent from her school friends. “I didn’t want to alienate anyone,” she said. “If everyone was singing along in the car to a Madonna song, I didn’t join in because when we’re younger we’re afraid of sticking out or showing off when in fact we should own those things that make us unique.”
