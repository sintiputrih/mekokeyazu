The Fabelmans star’s imitations of Mariah Carey and Justin Timberlake are a viral hit: fans say ‘nothing can prepare you’

    In (Hardcover £12.50 / $22.38 on Amazon) Britney tells all about everything, from her three marriages – and subsequent splits – to the termination of her 13-year conservatorship in 2021 and her troubled relationship with her parents and sister Jamie Lynn.

    But a pair of lighter moments - Britney meeting with Mariah Carey, and a hilarious telling of Justin Timberlake’s interaction with R&B star Ginuwine - have gone viral thanks to Michelle’s voicing of the anecdotes.

    Why did Michelle Williams narrate Britney's The Woman in Me book?

    While Britney herself voices a brief introduction to her autobiography, the full book is narrated by Michelle. As to why the award-winning actor agreed to provide a voice, literally, for the pop icon, Michelle simply said in a statement: “I stand with Britney.”

    Many are asking why Britney Spears didn’t narrate her own autobiography. “This book has been a labor of love and all the emotions that come with it,” the Toxic singer told People. “Reliving everything has been exciting, heart-wrenching and emotional, to say the least. For those reasons, I will only be reading a small part of my audiobook.

    “I am so grateful to the amazing Michelle Williams for reading the rest of it.”

    Britney meanwhile is celebrating the success of her new book, which has topped the bestseller list, writing on social media: “It’s happening  !!! My book is the highest selling celebrity memoir in history and it’s only day 1 !!! Thank you to the fans who have been so supportive !!! Love you all  !!! #TheWomaninMe @gallerybooks @simonandschuster."

    What is Amazon Audible - and how do I sign up?

    Audible is an Amazon membership service which grants customers access to a wide range of audiobooks, as well as podcasts, and magazine subscriptions. You can sign up for a 30-day free trial  - discover it here in the UK and here in the US - which entitles you one audiobook, or two audiobooks for Prime Members. The books do not expire, even after you cancel the trial.

    Audible can be synced to smart devices and shoppers can listen to their audiobook on the go on their smartphone, Fire tablet, Kindle, or Alexa device. 

    You also have access to a variety of genres, including sci-fi, romance fiction, biographies, and much more. 

    Once the trial is over, you will have to pay £7.99 ($14.95) a month to continue to have access to the best audiobooks. However, you can cancel any time.
