Early Life of Jude Law

On December 29, 1972, David Jude Heyworth Law was born in London, UK. He is Peter Robert Law and Margaret Anne Law’s son. His parents both held teaching positions at comprehensive schools; after a while, his father rose to the position of headmaster.

Law went to Kidbrooke School and John Ball Primary School. He eventually attended the Alleyn’s School, where he became interested in acting.
