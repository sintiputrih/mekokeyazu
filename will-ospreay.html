Professional Career of  Will Ospreay

Will began his career in the UK in 2012 on the independent circuit and is now considered as one of the top professional wrestlers in the world.

In 2016, he began working for the Japanese organization New Japan Pro Wrestling as a junior heavyweight after receiving AJ Styles’ recommendation.

It is all the more significant that AJ Styles was able to sign with New Japan Pro Wrestling in the manner he did because he was Will’s idol growing up.

Will Ospreay looked up to and was inspired by Styles, and the fact that he helped him get to New Japan ties them together.

Furthermore, Ospreay’s talent for hitting and his capacity to include high-flying elements into his technique do in some ways reflect AJ’s.

Will became the third IWGP Junior Heavyweight Champion after winning the Best of the Super Juniors in 2016 and 2019. Ospreay also captured the NEVER Openweight Championship in 2019.

The wrestler competed in the G1 Climax, the most amazing NJPW event for heavyweight wrestlers, that same year. In 2020, he changed weight classes and won the IWGP World Heavyweight Championship there.
