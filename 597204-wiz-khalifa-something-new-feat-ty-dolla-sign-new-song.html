Something New

Publish date: 2024-10-09

Fans have been patiently waiting for the release of Wiz Khalifa’s Rolling Papers 2 for the past couple years now. In fact, Wiz first announced the project back in May of 2015 which he later confirmed would be dropping in 2016, but as you know that unfortunately hasn't happened yet. So to hold us over in the meantime, the T.G.O.D. rapper has blessed us with two new mini EP's this Summer, Bong Rips & Pre-Rolleds, the latter of which featured Desiogner on the song “X 4 X 4.”

Having remained relatively quiet since then however, Wiz has been making his rounds on the site thanks to his features, lending assistance to Jr Donato & Ugly God (“No Lies”) recently, but thankfully that changes today. With no warning at all, the Pittsburgh native decides to link up with fellow T.G.O.D. crooner Ty Dolla Sign & drop off the new single “Something New.”

Its unclear at the moment if this will see life on Rolling Papers 2 or not, but it should definitely help ease our anticipation while we wait for updates.

Take a listen to the Yung Berg-produced single & let us know what you think. (Record available now on iTunes)
