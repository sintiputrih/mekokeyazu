Grantchester Season 6: Release Date, Cast, Trailer

Publish date: 2024-01-14

Grantchester is a British ITV detective drama, set in the 1950s Cambridgeshire village of the same name. The show first featured Anglican vicar, Sidney Chambers(James Norton) and subsequently vicar William Davenport (Tom Brittney), both of whom develop a sideline in sleuthing with the help of Detective Inspector Goldie Keating (Robson Green).

Grantchester is returning for series six with Robson Green, Kacey Ainsworth and Tom Brittney all reprising their roles in the hit period drama.

Release Date

Season 6 kicks off on Sunday, October 3. Viewers can tune into ITV at 9 pm for the hour-long episode. Fans of the show can enjoy a bit more of Granchester than there used to as series 6 is made up of 8 episodes instead of the usual 6. Making it the longest series so far.
