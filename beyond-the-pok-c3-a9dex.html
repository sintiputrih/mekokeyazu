Beyond the Pokédex, also known as Pokémon: Beyond the Pokédex[1], is a monthly web series created by The Pokémon Company International and uploaded on the official English Pokémon YouTube channel. Each episode focuses on one Pokémon; the series's hosts, Spruce and Maple, elaborate on the featured Pokémon and its Pokédex entries, as well as its appearances in various Pokémon media such as the games, anime, manga, and TCG.
