Levi Stubbs Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-02-01

Levi Stubbs was the lead vocalist of the Motown R&B group Four Tops and also worked as a voice artist in film and animated television series, famously voicing the alien plant Audrey II in the musical horror film. He passed away in 2008 in Detroit, Michigan at the age of 72. He was survived by his wife, Clineice Townsend, and their five children. Stubbs was known for his recognizable baritone voice and had a successful career with the Four Tops, signing with Motown Records in 1963 and producing 20 Top-40 hit songs over the next 10 years. He was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1990 as a member of The Four Tops. He was also known for his work as an actor and had notable roles in TV series and movies. He had a net worth of $7 million at the time of his passing.
