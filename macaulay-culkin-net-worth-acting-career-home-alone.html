After his celebrated role in Home Alone, Macaulay Culkin took a significant break from acting. Since then, sightings of him on screen have been rare, leaving fans curious about his well-being and financial stability. Yet, Culkin's current net worth reveals he's faring more than comfortably despite his prolonged absence from the spotlight.

Through diligent work in various ventures and wise financial management, Culkin has managed to sustain his wealth, maintaining a lifestyle that suits his preferences. This article will delve into Macaulay Culkin's net worth, his spending habits, and what he currently does.

How Much Is Macaulay Culkin's Net Worth Today?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Macaulay Culkin currently has a cool net worth of $18 million. This net worth most likely came about as a result of the wealth Culkin had acquired after amassing a great fortune during his peak days as a child actor. Culkin confessed to Ellen De Generes in 2018 that he never knew how much wealth he had amassed from acting till he turned 18 and was granted access to it. He said:

Macaulay Culkin's debut on the big screen was in 1989's Uncle Buck, for which he received $40,000. However, it was his iconic portrayal of Kevin McCallister in the 1990 blockbuster Home Alone that catapulted him to stardom.

Despite the film's immense success, Culkin was initially paid $100,000 for his role, a modest sum considering its impact. Nevertheless, his reprisal of the character in the 1992 sequel, Home Alone 2: Lost in New York, proved more lucrative, earning him approximately $4.5 million.

Following his breakout in Home Alone, Culkin became a sought-after child actor, securing various roles from 1990 to 1994 that significantly boosted his earnings. Despite not receiving any royalties from Home Alone, Culkin has demonstrated prudent financial management, ensuring that his earnings from the film and subsequent projects sustain his livelihood.

In 2018, Macaulay Culkin was paid a whopping $1 million by Google for featuring in a Google Assistance advert where he recreated part of his role as Kevin, in Home Alone.

How Macaulay Culkin Spends His Net Worth

Macaulay Culkin spends his wealth mostly on real estate. In the early 2000s, Culkin made some wise real estate investments that met his desire for a good life as well as played a significant role in boosting his net worth. He spent his money on purchasing some apartments in New York and Paris during that period. Culkin's present New York City apartment was bought within that same period.

In 2022, Culkin and his partner Brenda Song made headlines by purchasing Keifer Sutherland's sprawling 4,879 square-foot home in Toluca, Los Angeles, for $8 million. The historic property, originally built in 1935, boasts five bedrooms, six bathrooms, and spans across 6 acres, featuring luxurious amenities such as a swimming pool, a terrace with a fireplace, and a putting green.

What Is Macaulay Culkin Doing Now?

Despite expressing contentment with his financial situation in a 2016 interview with The Guardian, Macaulay Culkin has remained committed to staying active rather than being idle.
