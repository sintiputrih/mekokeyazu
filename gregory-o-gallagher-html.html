Gregory O'Gallagher - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Content creator and fitness guru known for his company, Kinobody, which offers exercise routines and nutrition guidance aimed at achieving a lean, muscular body. His YouTube channel, Kinobody, has amassed 700,000 subscribers.

Before Fame

He studied business and marketing at the University of Guelph in Canada but dropped out after a year to launch Kinobody. He went viral in 2015 after posting a YouTube video entitled "The Real Bruce Wayne," one of many videos in which he impersonates old-school movie heroes. 

Trivia

He worked as a personal trainer after moving to Los Angeles when he was 19 years old. He went on to found Kino Clothing, which offers workout gear and athleisure. 

Family Life

His father, real estate mogul Michael O'Gallagher, died when he was 11. His mother is Alanne O'Gallagher. He is the second oldest of four siblings. 

Associated With

Model and YouTuber Chase Mattson posts similar content.
