Who Is Trudy Busch Valentine Running For Senate In Missouri?

Trudy Busch Valentine is the heiress of Busch Beer Empire. She has recently announced that she is running for Senate in Missouri.

According to U.S. News, her entry into the campaign comes just days after Republican candidate Eric Greitens was accused of physically abusing his family members. The GOP attempts to avoid nominating a damaged candidate who risks handing the seat to a Democrat.

Talking about Valentine, she has worked on nonprofit boards and in organizations dedicated to children, education, environmental protection, health care, and the nursing profession, as mentioned on the family’s website.

She remains associated with the Saint Louis University School of Nursing and the University’s board of trustees while describing herself as a nurse at heart.

Trudy Busch Valentine Comes From The Family That Owns Busch Beer Empire

Trudy Busch Valentine belongs to the family that owns Busch Beer Empire. Her father is the late Gussie Busch. She has a sibling named Billy Busch Sr. Her mother was the third wife of Gussie.

However, her father had ten children, and the eight others are her step-siblings. Besides her blood-related family members, she also has her husband and kids.

She was previously married to John Valentine and had six children with him. However, fate played games with her as she was widowed.

But, she found love in another man who was also widowed. Her current partner is John Fries. They have eight children and eight grandchildren altogether.

How Much Is Trudy Busch Net Worth?

Trudy Busch has not revealed her net worth details yet. However, she is a nurse by profession but also handles her family business of Busch Beer.

Moreover, in the recent video, she disclosed her finances and charity work, which shows that she is wealthy. As mentioned in SLT Post, she has raised a lot of money for Democratic politicians.

In 2016, she hosted a fundraiser for Democratic presidential contender Hillary Clinton at Grant’s Farm, the historic homestead of the Busch family. Likewise, she has donated millions worth of money to Saint Louis University.
